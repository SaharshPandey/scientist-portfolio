Important Works and Awards


  • Successful completion of the three International Bilateral project as a Principle Investigator.

  • Successful organization of the rst Belgo-Indian Network on Astronomy and Astrophysics (BINA) workshop at ARIES Nainital from 15 to 18 November 2016.

  • Discovery of the milli-magnitude pulsations in eight chemically peculiar stars.

  • Discovery of thousands of new variable stars using Kepler archival data.

  • Discovery of the rst giant double radio relic PLCKG287.0+32.9 in a Galaxy Cluster.

  • Discovery of the largest, unusual, highly asymmetric radio galaxy jet.

  • Successful commissioning of the Fast Photometric System for 1.04-m and 1.3-m telescopes at ARIES.
